SAT/TOEFL Preparation

One of the most important things we do at Ghana Soccer Education Network is educate and prepare prospective student athletes for...
Learn More

One of the most important things we do at Ghana Soccer Education Network is educate and prepare prospective student athletes for the SAT/TOEFL. By educating and preparing the high school athletes and their families about the sports recruiting process and the importance of the SAT/ACT/TOEFL, we enhance their chances of receiving college athletic scholarship offers.We will accomplish this service by providing organized classes and after school programs run by our standardized test expert, Mrs. Mairlyn. With access to preparation books and teaching methods otherwise unavailable to these student athletes, these classes and after school programs will allow them more opportunity to better their education, enhance their chances of receiving academic scholarships, and ultimately better their lives.

Organize Showcase Games

Learn More

Showcase games are organized regularly in Ghana throughout the year. It provides a good opportunity for prospective student athletes in Ghana and other African countries to audition their talent in front of several American College Coaches. GHSEN will allow college coaches looking to visit West Africa an efficient and effective opportunity to see prospective student athletes playing in person. Showcase games serve as a platform where most talents are scouted throughout the country, so it is a great opportunity for college coaches to see a wealth of talent of display.
